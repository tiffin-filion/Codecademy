About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio! I have a Bachelor's in Web Design that is outdated, so I'm currently updating my skills. My uncle felt having a computer was important, so I've had one at home since I was 2. Typing "RUN" was the norm for awhile. Then when the internet became mainstream, he felt we needed access to that too. So I taught myself HTML 1.0 to create a website by myself. I love to learn and I love to create and test things, so Software Developer is the perfect job for me. Also, I was a QA Analyst for websites & emails for 13 years, so I feel I have a bit of an advantage on testing what I create.
