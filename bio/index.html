I have a Bachelor's Degree in Web Development (which is outdated), so I'm updating my skills. Currently I'm attending Skillcrush for Web Development and am in the process of enrolling in a Front-End Developer Certificate program at Highline College.

My uncle felt having a computer was important, so I've had one at home since I was 2. Then when the internet became mainstream, he felt we needed access to that too. So I taught myself HTML 1.0 to create a website by myself.

I love to learn and I love to create and test things, so Front-End Developer is the perfect job for me. Also, I was a Quality Assurance Engineer for websites & emails for the past 13 years, so I feel I have a bit of an advantage on testing what I create.
